Dr. Monehela "Nelly" Molapo

Lead Dentist & Founder

Dr. Monehela "Nelly" Molapo founded Dr MC Molapo Dentist in 2016 with a vision to create a practice where patients feel like family. With 22 years of experience in general and cosmetic dentistry, she brings a wealth of knowledge and a gentle touch to every procedure. Dr. Molapo values and respects her patients, believing in staying at the forefront of dental technology while maintaining the personal care that makes each patient feel valued.

Education & Training

  • Bachelor of Dental Surgery, Sefako Health Sciences University (Formerly MEDUNSA)
  • Bachelor of Science, National University of Lesotho (NUL)
  • 22 Years of Professional Experience

Specializations

  • General Dentistry
  • Preventive Care
  • Patient-Centered Care
